# Products Module

## Overview

The Product (Item) module allows businesses to manage their product catalog and track inventory, revenue, and sales performance.

## Accessing Products

Click **Item** (📦 icon) in the left navigation sidebar.

## Product KPIs

Upon entering the Product module, these KPI cards are displayed:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|------------|
| **Current Stock** | Total units currently in stock |
| **Inventory Value** | Monetary value of current inventory (₹) |
| **Total Revenue** | Revenue generated from product sales |
| **Units Sold** | Total units sold |

![Product Data Entry View](/screenshots/products/10_Data_Entry_View_enhanced.jpg)

## Empty State

If no products have been added yet, the system shows:

> "No products added yet. Add products to start tracking sales insights."

## Adding Products

1. Click the **Add Product** button
2. Fill in the product details (name, price, stock quantity, etc.)
3. Click **Save** to add the product to your catalog

> 💡 **Tip:** Add all products before tracking sales to ensure accurate KPI calculations.
