# Login

## Overview

ZoTok uses a web-based authentication system. Users log in with their credentials to access the platform dashboard and all modules.

## How to Log In

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the ZoTok platform URL provided by your administrator.
2. Enter your **username** and **password** in the login form.
3. Click **Sign In**.

## After Login

After successful authentication, you are greeted with the **Home Dashboard** displaying:

- A welcome message with your firm name (e.g., "Hello, Hd Agencies")
- The navigation sidebar on the left with access to all core modules
- Key performance indicators and data tables in the main content area

## Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|---------|
| Forgotten password | Contact your administrator to reset |
| Account locked | Wait 15 minutes or contact support |
| Browser compatibility | Use the latest Chrome, Firefox, or Edge |
